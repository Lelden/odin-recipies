Banana Bread

Cassava Flour Banana Bread

This recipe is often cooked at our house, as we have food issues with gluten. My wife cooks this often in the evening to have a loaf to wake up to.

Ingredients

  • 3 bananas
  • 3 eggs
  • 1/2 cup of sugar
  • 1/2 cup of water
  • 1 1/2 cups of cassava flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking soda
  • Shake of cinnamon
  1. Mix cassava flour, cinnamon, and baking soda in a small bowl. Set aside.
  2. Mash 3 bananas in a big bowl. Add eggs and whisk together. Mix in sugar and water. Add dry mixture to wet, and mix together well.
  3. Oil bread pan with coconut oil or avocado oil. Pour batter into pan.
  4. Cook at 350 for 40 minutes.
